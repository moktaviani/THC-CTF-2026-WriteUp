# THC CTF 2026 WriteUp

Welcome to the **THC CTF 2026 WriteUp Repository**.

This repository contains writeups and challenge solutions from several challenges that I solved during the **THC CTF 2026 (THCon Capture The Flag)** competition. The writeups are intended for educational purposes, knowledge sharing, and helping others learn cybersecurity techniques used in CTF environments.

---

## Toulouse Hacking Convention (THCon)

**THCon (Toulouse Hacking Convention)** is one of the largest cybersecurity conferences in France, bringing together cybersecurity enthusiasts, students, professionals, researchers, and industry experts.

Since its creation in **2017**, THCon has grown into a major international cybersecurity event held annually in **Toulouse, France**, attracting participants from around the world.
